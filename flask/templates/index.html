Under the hood

A transparent, lightweight model

Cardia uses a K-Nearest Neighbours classifier trained on standardized cardiac patient records. Inputs are scaled with the same StandardScaler used during training, then compared against the model's learned neighbourhood to classify risk as high or low.

  • Same feature engineering as the original research notebook
  • Deterministic, reproducible preprocessing pipeline
  • No data leaves your session
